Disparity Among NYC Middle School Test Takers Prevalent

The number of New York City middle-school students taking the English Language And Arts Test is a healthy number, but a distinct patterns amongst borough based performance raises larger questions

By Adwait


Students



School districts 31 (Staten Island) 24 (Queens) lead the the city in the total number of students who take the English Language And Arts Test in New York City.
Both districts have an average of above 26K students each year over the past five years.

School district 16 (Brooklyn) has the smallest average number of test takers in the same time period.




Scores




District 26 (Queens) and 2 (Manhattan) lead the average test scores in New Yok City.

District's 7, 9 and 12 (Bronx) have the lowest average test scores in the past five years.
